Girish Nivarti

Research Scientist

I work at the School of Mathematics, University of Leeds. My work involves computational simulation of complex physical systems, with a focus on fluid flow phenomena.

I develop mathematical models and softwares that enable simulations, and I also analyse simulation data to decipher the mechanisms relevant to the physics of the problem at hand.

Currently, I am building a Direct Statistical Simulation framework aimed at predicting zonal mean flows in planetary atmospheres.

As part of the Leeds Institute for Fluid Dynamics, I founded the Fluids Forum for early career researchers in fluids at Leeds.
